# DailyNote — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 22 July 2026_

DailyNote does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

- **Your notes** are plain text files in a folder you choose on your device or
  iCloud Drive. DailyNote reads and writes those files locally. They are never
  sent anywhere by the app.
- **No accounts.** The app has no sign-up, sign-in, or user identifiers.
- **No analytics or tracking.** The app contains no analytics, advertising, or
  third-party SDKs, and makes no network connections.
- **Folder access** is granted by you through Apple's document picker and can
  be revoked at any time by deleting the app or choosing a different folder.
- **iCloud sync** of your notes folder, if any, is performed by Apple's iCloud
  Drive under your Apple account and Apple's privacy policy; DailyNote simply
  edits files inside that folder.

Questions: open an issue on this repository.
